Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

At Send-Bills.com, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our free invoice generator service.

1. Information We Collect

Send-Bills.com is designed with privacy at its core. We want to be transparent about how our service works:

  • Invoice Data: All invoice data you enter (business names, addresses, item descriptions, amounts) is processed entirely in your web browser. This data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions properly. We also use third-party advertising cookies (such as Google AdSense) to serve relevant advertisements. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Analytics Data: We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymized usage data including pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location. This helps us improve our service.
  • Log Data: Our web servers may automatically collect standard log information such as your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps. This data is used for security and maintenance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our free invoice generator service
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements through our advertising partners
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our website

3. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense and potentially other advertising networks to display ads on our website. These third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Since all invoice creation happens locally in your browser, your invoice data is never stored on our servers. When you close your browser tab or navigate away from Send-Bills.com, your invoice data is automatically removed from your browser's memory. We do not have access to any invoices you create.

5. Children's Privacy

Send-Bills.com is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

Since we do not store your invoice data, most data we hold is limited to analytics and server logs. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email provided on our Contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Cookie Policy GDPR CCPA Contact page or email us at privacy@send-bills.com.
