Our Story

Pushpender Sodlan

Founder & Developer

Built Send-Bills.com to solve a real problem — creating professional invoices should be instant, free, and require zero sign-up.

Send-Bills.com was born from a real frustration. Our founder, Pushpender Sodlan, was running his own business when he needed to send a single invoice to a client. Something that simple shouldn't have been difficult — but it was. Every tool he found wanted him to create an account, enter a credit card, start a "14-day free trial," or pay $15-50/month for features he'd never use.

That experience hit hard. It wasn't just inconvenient — it was slowing his business down. He lost time, he lost momentum, and he realized millions of freelancers, contractors, and small business owners face the exact same pain every day. Invoicing was being gatekept behind paywalls and forced sign-ups when it should be as simple as writing an email.

So Pushpender built the tool he wished existed. A platform where anyone can create a professional invoice in 60 seconds, send it to their client, accept payments, and manage their entire billing — without ever creating an account or paying a cent.

Our Mission

We believe invoicing is a basic business need, not a luxury. It should be as free and accessible as email. Send-Bills.com exists to remove every barrier between you and getting paid — no sign ups, no paywalls, no complexity. That's not a marketing line — it's the founding principle Pushpender built this entire platform on.

How It Works

Send-Bills.com is a client-side invoice generator. This means everything happens right in your web browser — your data never leaves your device. Simply choose your document type (Invoice, Bill, Receipt, Estimate, or Quotation), fill in your business details, add your client's information, list your items or services, add notes and terms & conditions, and download a professionally formatted PDF. It is that simple.

How We Stay Free — Forever

We operate on four principles that keep Send-Bills.com free without compromising your experience:

Non-intrusive advertising — Tasteful ads from trusted partners help cover our infrastructure costs. We never interrupt your workflow or put ads inside your invoices.

Client-side architecture — Your invoices are created in your browser, not on our servers. This keeps our operating costs extremely low, which means we don't need to charge you.

Community growth — When you share Send-Bills.com with a colleague or friend, that's our marketing. Happy users are our best advocates.

No venture capital pressure — We're not backed by investors demanding aggressive monetization. We built this to solve a real problem, not to extract value from the people we serve.
