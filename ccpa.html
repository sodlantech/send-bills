CCPA/CPRA Privacy Rights

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Send-Bills.com complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). This notice explains what personal information we collect from California residents, how we use it, and the rights available to you under California law.

2. Categories of Personal Information Collected

We collect the following categories of personal information from California residents:

  • Identifiers: Name, email address, business name, phone number, and IP address.
  • Commercial Information: Invoice details, payment information, and purchase history.
  • Internet Activity: Browsing history, pages visited, time spent on site, clicks, and referral information.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Location Information: Approximate geographic location based on IP address.
  • Sensory Information: None collected.
  • Inferences: We may derive inferences about your preferences and interests based on your usage patterns to improve our service.

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information collected for the following business purposes:

  • Providing and improving our invoice generator service
  • Personalizing user experience and content
  • Responding to customer inquiries and support requests
  • Marketing and advertising (with your consent where required)
  • Analytics and measuring website performance
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA

California residents have the following privacy rights:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request what personal information we collect, use, and share about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. We will not deny, charge different prices for, or provide different quality of service based on your exercise of CCPA rights.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You have the right to limit our use of sensitive personal information to purposes necessary to provide our service.

5. How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of your CCPA/CPRA rights, you may submit a request by emailing us at privacy@send-bills.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." Include sufficient information to identify you and describe your request clearly.

We will verify your identity before processing your request. We may ask for additional information if needed to confirm your identity or understand your request. We will respond to your verified request within 45 days, or notify you if we need additional time (up to 90 days total).

6. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their commercial benefit. We do not engage in the sale of personal information as defined under CCPA/CPRA. Your personal information is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve our service.

7. Financial Incentives

We do not offer financial incentives, discounts, or other benefits in exchange for personal information. We do not have a loyalty or rewards program that affects the price or quality of our service based on personal information.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice, unless a longer retention period is required by law. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, subject to certain legal exceptions.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this CCPA/CPRA notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any significant updates on this page and update the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this notice periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this notice, your privacy rights, or to submit a privacy request, please contact us at privacy@send-bills.com or visit our Contact page.
