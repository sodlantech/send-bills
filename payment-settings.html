How Online Payments Work

  1. Connect your Stripe or PayPal account above (use your own keys — we never touch your money)
  2. Create an invoice as usual and download/send it
  3. Share the invoice link with your client — it includes a "Pay Now" button
  4. Get paid — money goes directly to your Stripe/PayPal account. Invoice status auto-updates to "Paid"

Send-Bills.com never has access to your funds. Payments flow directly from your client to your payment account. Standard Stripe (2.9% + 30c) or PayPal fees apply.
