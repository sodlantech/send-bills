Frequently Asked Questions

Is Send-Bills.com really free?
Yes, Send-Bills.com is 100% free to use. There are no hidden charges, premium tiers, or subscription fees. We are supported by advertisements.

Do I need to create an account?
No. You can create and download invoices instantly without any registration, login, or sign up process.

Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All invoice data is processed locally in your web browser. We never see, store, or transmit any of your business or client information. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use Send-Bills.com for my business?
Yes, our invoice generator is suitable for freelancers, small businesses, contractors, and entrepreneurs. However, please ensure the invoices meet your local tax and legal requirements.

What currencies are supported?
We support 49+ currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, CNY, AUD, CAD, BRL, PHP, CHF, SEK, NOK, DKK, SGD, HKD, KRW, TWD, TRY, ZAR, MYR, THB, AED, SAR, PKR, BDT, MXN, and many more.

Can I create documents other than invoices?
Yes! Send-Bills.com supports five document types: Invoice, Bill, Receipt, Estimate, and Quotation. Simply select the type from the dropdown at the top of the form.

Can I add terms and conditions to my invoice?
Yes. There is a dedicated Terms & Conditions section below the Notes field where you can add your custom terms, which will appear on both the live preview and the downloaded PDF.
